Our Mission

To stimulate curiosity through hands-on experiences with interactive educational exhibits and working equipment displays set with period furniture and design.    

About

The Museum will include rotating exhibits of audio, video, communication and other technological advancements from the 20th century; other areas of the museum will focus on the inventors and scientists who contributed to the technological expansion of the last century. 

The Resource Center library includes documentation, manuals and brochures for technology in addition to books, magazines and other pertinent literature. 

The Learning Arcade will include a computer lab and workshop for classes and student field trips as well as interactive activities centered on the technology. Other installations will allow the guests to create, build and design elements for an attraction, demystifying the technology along the way.
