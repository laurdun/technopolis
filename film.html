Bolex 3D

A somewhat unusual camera, the Bolex 3D shoots a side-by-side format on 16mm film. The projection lens is also part of our collection along with film shot in this format.

Below is our Moviola Series 20 film editor from the 1950s.

Many other film artifacts will be on display including a high-speed 70mm camera from NASA and a flat-bed Videola transfer system for 16mm and 35mm.
