Bolex 3D

A somewhat unusual camera, the Bolex 3D shoots a side-by-side format on 16mm film. The projection lens is also part of our collection along with film shot in this format.







Moviola Series 20 

This film editor is from the 1950s where is was "the way" to edit motion picture film.

Left side for sound











Photo-Sonics 10A

A high-speed 70mm film camera from NASA capable of running 300 frames per second.









The high-speed internal parts include a rotating shutter and claw.

The exhibit runs much more slowly to demonstrate the precision mechanism.
