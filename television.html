Light to Heavy

The Panasonic TR-001, one of the lightest televisions made, has a 1-inch screen, and on November 14, 1969, seven people watched the launch of Apollo 12 on this very television.

The Ampex VR-3000 video tape recorder, one of the heaviest portable machines, weights around 50 pounds; cameramen used to carry this machine on their backs for recording outside the studio. The tape is 2 inches wide, and one reel of tape records for 20 minutes.

We also have a 1939 Iconoscope camera.




Magnetic Tape

The giant reel-to-reel Quadruplex 2-inch-wide tape could capture an a hour and a half of broadcast recording; this artifact contains an actual broadcast from 1980. Look close - there is a one-hour HDTV cassette in front of the reel.

We have quite an assortment of media to exhibit.
